About

Are you someone who shares my passion for building thriving communities and making a positive impact on the world around us?

Let me introduce myself. In 2015, I founded a business from scratch and successfully scaled it within a year. Following that, I transitioned to working as a graphic designer for four years before discovering my true calling in community building. Since then, I've been building multiple communities and hosting online and offline events, including global events, as part of the Community Folks.team.

I'm also a founding team member of a travel startup called "Travander." As part of the team, I helped develop a clear business model, design a go-to-market strategy, identify the right demographic, create a community strategy, and launch it with internal members.

But the truth is, community building has been a part of me all along. Even before I knew the formal terminology for it, I was creating successful gaming communities on Discord and expanding them over time by delegating responsibilities to other passionate users.

My ultimate goal is to bring together like-minded individuals and foster a strong sense of community, while also delivering significant business value.

Helping others and bringing people together is what truly motivates me, and I'm always just a ping away! Oh, and did I mention that I'm also a huge animal lover with a soft spot for creatures big and small, whether they reside on land, in the mountains, or in the sea and sky?

According to me,

Community Builders are the Unsung Superheroes who don’t wear capes.
