About

Hi! I love building communities and contributing in them, do you also?

A little about me: After building a business from scratch in 2015 and scaling it to success within a year, working as a freelance graphic designer for almost 4 years, I discovered the world of community building.

Although I had been building communities long before that, without knowing the formal terminology for it. On Discord, I started gaming communities and effectively expanded them over time, handing the reins off to other engaged users at later stage.

Helping people and bringing them together is what motivates me.

I am a big time zoophilist, from smallest to largest, from land to mountains and from sea to sky, I love them all.

And I am always just a ping away!

According to me,

Community Builders are the Unsung Superheroes who don’t wear capes.​​​​​​
